About Zibo Unique

Zibo Unique supplies stainless steel food processing machinery and processing line solutions for practical factory production.

Practical Machinery for Food Production

Zibo Unique focuses on equipment for washing, peeling, cutting, drying, conveying and food processing applications. The machines are used by food factories, seafood processors, fruit and vegetable processors and central kitchens that need efficient and hygienic preparation steps.

Our team provides equipment selection support, layout suggestions and technical guidance based on each customer's raw material, capacity and processing requirements.

Engineering Support

Selection support starts from the customer's raw material, target output, process steps and site conditions.

Factory-Direct Supply

Zibo Unique can support both single-machine purchases and customized processing line combinations.

Export Preparation
