About This Buyer Guide

This site is designed as an overseas application guide and inquiry support resource for food processing machinery buyers.

Application Guidance Before Quotation

This website helps buyers prepare machine selection and quotation information before contacting the sales team. It focuses on practical application questions: raw material type, process flow, capacity, factory layout, voltage, water supply, drainage and final product requirements.

Official product supply and final quotation are confirmed by the authorized sales team. This site does not replace the official company website or final technical confirmation.

What This Site Helps With

  • Application-based machinery selection
  • Processing line planning questions
  • Quotation preparation checklists
  • WhatsApp and email inquiry support

For Overseas Buyers

Content is written for buyers in the Middle East, Southeast Asia, Europe and Latin America who need clear English guidance before requesting a catalog or quotation.

For Practical Planning

Guides explain washing, peeling, cutting, drying, conveying and processing line decisions without inventing unsupported specifications.

For Inquiry Support

Buyers can send raw material, capacity and layout details by WhatsApp or email for more patient one-to-one communication.
