FAQ

What shrimp information is needed before quotation?

Please provide shrimp type, head-on or headless condition, size range, peeling style, deveining requirement and required hourly capacity.

Can shrimp peeling equipment connect with washing and conveying lines?

Yes. Machine selection can consider upstream washing, water supply, drainage, inspection, conveying and downstream packing preparation.

Who uses industrial seafood processing machines?

Typical users include seafood processing plants, frozen shrimp factories, ready-to-cook seafood suppliers and central kitchens.
