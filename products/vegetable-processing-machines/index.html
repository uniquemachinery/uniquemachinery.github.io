FAQ

Which vegetable machine should a factory choose first?

Start by confirming the raw material, soil level, cutting shape, required hourly capacity and whether the final product needs dewatering or drying before packing.

Can one line process different vegetables?

Some line sections can handle multiple vegetables, but cutting tools, washing intensity and dewatering methods may need adjustment for each product.

What should buyers provide before quotation?

Buyers should provide raw material photos, target cut size, capacity, factory layout, voltage, water supply, drainage and packing method.
