Machine Selection Guide

This page helps buyers compare machine categories and prepare quotation details. For the official product catalog and final quotation, please contact the authorized sales team.

Compare by Application and Raw Material

Use these categories as a selection guide, not a complete product catalog. The right configuration depends on raw material, soil level, processing target, required capacity, utilities and factory layout.

Vegetable Washing Machines

Best for: leafy vegetables, root vegetables, fresh-cut vegetables and pre-washing before cutting.

Typical raw materials: lettuce, cabbage, spinach, carrots, potatoes and mixed vegetables.

Prepare: vegetable type, soil level, capacity, water circulation need and factory space.

Read guide | Ask for advice

Vegetable Cutting Machines

Best for: central kitchens, ready-meal plants and fresh-cut suppliers preparing uniform product sizes.

Typical raw materials: leafy vegetables, root vegetables, fruit and prepared ingredients.

Prepare: final cut size, raw material dimensions, upstream washing process and output target.

Plan line | Ask for advice

Seafood Processing Machines

Best for: shrimp peeling, deveining, seafood washing and ready-to-cook seafood preparation.

Typical raw materials: shrimp and seafood products that require washing or handling before packing.

Prepare: shrimp size, peeling style, deveining need, capacity, water supply and drainage.

Read checklist | Ask for advice

Meat Processing Machines

Best for: factories and food service production rooms that need stainless steel preparation equipment.

Typical raw materials: meat ingredients for cutting, mixing, preparation or handling steps.

Prepare: material form, hygiene requirements, batch size and target process.

Ask for advice

Potato Processing Machines

Best for: potato washing, peeling, inspection, cutting and transfer before packing or cooking.

Typical raw materials: potatoes and similar root vegetables.

Prepare: potato size range, peeling requirement, cut shape, capacity and water/drainage conditions.

Read potato guide | Ask for advice

Central Kitchen Equipment

Best for: ready-meal plants and central kitchens building repeatable preparation steps.

Typical raw materials: vegetables, prepared ingredients and mixed production batches.

Prepare: menu categories, daily output, cleaning rules, available space and packing preparation needs.

Read central kitchen guide

Packing and Weighing Machines

Best for: preparing processed materials for weighing, bagging or packing after washing/cutting.

Typical raw materials: vegetables, seafood and prepared ingredients.

Prepare: package weight, product moisture, weighing accuracy target and upstream process.

Ask for advice

Complete Processing Lines

Best for: buyers who need several machines connected into one practical production flow.

Typical raw materials: vegetables, shrimp, potatoes, salads and central kitchen ingredients.

Prepare: raw material input, process flow, capacity, layout drawing, voltage and packing goal.
