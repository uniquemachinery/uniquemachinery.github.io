Privacy Policy

This policy explains how Unique Machinery handles information from B2B machinery inquiries.

Last updated: May 26, 2026

Information We Collect

When you contact us, we may collect your name, email address, WhatsApp or phone number, country, product interest, required capacity and message details.

Inquiry Form Information

The inquiry form is used to prepare a message for WhatsApp or email. The website does not require an account and does not run a backend database on GitHub Pages.

Email and WhatsApp Communication

If you send an inquiry by email or WhatsApp, your message and contact details are used to reply to your request, understand your production needs and provide equipment selection or quotation support.

How We Use Information

We use inquiry information to communicate with buyers, recommend suitable food processing machinery, prepare quotations and provide technical guidance related to the requested equipment.

No Sale of Personal Data

We do not sell personal information. We do not use inquiry information for unrelated advertising services.

Basic Data Protection

We aim to handle buyer inquiry information carefully and limit access to people who need it for sales, quotation and technical support communication.

Contact

For privacy questions or contact information updates, email Miameng@zibounique.com.
